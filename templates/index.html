DC-Data

This application combines local news coverage and weather data of the D.C./Baltimore Area.

  • Search for a word or phrase and see how often it was mentioned by the local newstations over time.
  • Clicking on a day in the calendar will display news clips from that day containing the current word/phrase.
  • All weather data is in blue
  • Drag around the blue-square-brush on the top chart to zoom in on the bottom charts below.

To get started "Flash Flood" has been entered for you already. Try and find out why it was mentioned so many times between July and October of 2011 and if any of the weather metrics correlate.
